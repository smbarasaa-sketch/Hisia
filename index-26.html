🏛️ Know Your Rights

📍 Nairobi County Menstrual Leave Policy (Dec 2025) County government employees are entitled to 2 paid menstrual leave days per month — no questions asked, no forms to fill. This is on top of sick and annual leave.
⚖️ Article 43 — Constitution of Kenya 2010 You have the constitutional right to the highest attainable standard of health, including reproductive health care. This is your right as a Kenyan.
📜 Sanitary Towels Bill 2024 (Pending) This bill, currently awaiting enactment, seeks to provide free quality sanitary towels in all public institutions in Kenya.
